How does an AI make a sentence?

  • AIs build sentences by putting words next to each other one by one
  • They copy ways they've seen words appear together before
  • They predict what word comes next in a sequence

Can AIs be wrong?

  • AIs make predictions based on patterns
  • They don't "know" facts, they predict probabilities
  • They can be confidently incorrect
  • They reflect biases in their training data

Can humans be wrong?

  • Yes, humans make mistakes
  • We have biases and blind spots
  • We can be overconfident
  • We sometimes believe things that aren't true

How do we know if something is true?

  • Evidence and observation
  • Expert opinion
  • Critical thinking and skepticism
